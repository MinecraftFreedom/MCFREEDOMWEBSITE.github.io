Discover our server

Delve in depth what this server can provide to you

Community Projects

We plan and insentivise community projects, involving multiple people to come together to produce a common output.Sections of our spawn is reserved for common community projects , such as the event section and shopping district, aswell as many future communal buildings.

Frequent Events

We enjoy offering many ways to play minecraft with the community, since our servers vanilla experience is Non PvP , however we are committed to provdiding external game-mode based events , these events are either picked by staff or by the community , these events are not limited only on the server, sometimes we may play other games or engage in other events.
All events are open to everyone ,bringing the community together and being intertwined.

Helping Community

We are committed to ensuring all players feel welcomed and have access to plethora of resources and buildings that can assist you on your journey here, using navigation tools such as the dynmap to assist finding helpful locations, or simply asking any friendly members for assistance in game or on discord.

Trade and Faith

Our server provides no formal protection system , while the ability to grief and steal is a possibility , any attempts will result in a restoration and a ban.Putting faith into people we think strengthens a community.Further, we operate designated section of spawn for shops , a simple system using chests and signs to indicate the price and items being sold.Trade is crucial for us as it promotes collaboration and allows for switfer progress in your experience.

This is a current brief of everything our server currently has to offer, we are actively open to suggestions that would better the experience for our players.

Join Us:

To join us , you will need to undergo and submit an application , we will only except sumbissons of exceptional quality, if you think this server is right for you, we suggest taking considerable time and effort into filling out an application , be consise , clear and why you think you would be of value and a great inclusion to the community.
We only give one chance with the application , if said application is rejected , we will not except any future applications.
