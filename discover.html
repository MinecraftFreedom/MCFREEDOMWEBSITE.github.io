Discover our server

Delve deep into what our server offers to you

Community Projects

We plan and incentivize community projects involving multiple peoples' collaboration. Sections of our spawn are reserved for common community projects, such as the event section and the shopping district, as well as many future communal buildings.

Frequent Events

We enjoy offering many ways to experience Minecraft as a community. Our server's vanilla experience is non PvP, however we are committed to providing external game mode-based events, picked by staff or by the community. Such events are not limited to being hosted on the server, but can also be for other games or other events. All events are open to everyone, bringing our community together.

Helping Community

We are committed to ensuring all players feel welcomed and have access to the plethora of resources and buildings available to assist you on your journey here. We have navigation tools such as the Dynmap to assist you in finding helpful locations, or you can simply ask any of our friendly members for assistance, in-game or via Discord.

Trade and Faith

We believe that putting faith and trust into our people strengthens a community. As such, our server provides no formal protection system. However, although griefing and stealing is possible, any attempts will result in a restoration and a perpetrator ban. For trade, we have a designated section of spawn for the shopping district with a simple system using chests and signs to indicate the prices of items being sold. Trade is an integral part of our server as it promotes collaboration and allows for faster progress in your server experience.

This is a current brief of everything our server currently has to offer, we are actively open to suggestions that would better the experience for our players.

Join Us:

To join us, you will need to submit an application. We only accept submissions of exceptional quality. If you think this server is right for you, take your time filling out the application. Be concise and explain why you think you would be a great, valuable addition to our community. You will have one chance to fill out an application. If it is rejected, we will not accept any further applications. Thank you for your interest, and we hope to see you!
