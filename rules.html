General Rules:

General Rules of MinecraftFreedom

► Advertisements:
● Permitted - You are allowed to advertise any and all media related to MinecraftFreedom
● Not Permitted - You are not allowd to advertise anything non related to MinecraftFreedom , this included but not limited to IP addresses , media from other servers , encouraging players to join or play other servers.Advertising your own social media or other social links is also not permitted.


► Abusing Exploits/ Bugs:
● Not Permitted - You are not allowed to willingly abuse bugs or exploits in any situation.


► Scamming:
● Not Permitted - You are not allowed to scam players for anything in all circumstances.


► IRL DEALS:
● Not Permitted - You are not allowed to sell ingame items or currency for real currency.


► Evading Punishments:
● Not Permitted - You are not allowed to use an alternate account to evade a punative punishment.


► Ticket & /report Abuse:
● Not Permitted - The abuse of these features to request Staff Help is not allowed.You must and only send one per message.


► Impersonating:
● Not Permitted - You are not allowed to impersonate players or staff.


► Lying:
● Not Permitted - Lying to staff under any circumstances is not allowed.


► Inappropriate Names , ItemNames , Skins & Usernames:
● Not Permitted - You are not allowed to change or have any of the following that would be considered 18+ or offensive.


► Illegal Items:
● Permitted - Any unobtainable items that have been rewarded to players during events are allowed.
● Not Permitted - Any items that are not obtainable in-game are not allowed.


► Illegal Activity:
● Not Permitted - Under any circumstances , you are niot allowed to do anything that is considered illegal.


► Alternate Accounts:
● Not Permitted - No more than one alternate account is allowed.


► Griefing:
● Not Permitted - Creations such as lava casts or other obstructing creations are not allowed to be built.


► Killing/Trapping:
● Not Permitted - Under any circumstances , killing players outside of /warp PvP , without a bounty & trapping is not allowed.


► AFK Fishing:
● Not Permitted - No use of contraptions or modifications that allow you to AFK Fish.


► Death Messages:
● Not Permitted - Repeatedly dying on purpose to flood chat.


► Casions:
● Not Permitted - Under any form we do not allow the operation of player based casinos.


► Farms:
● Players can build their farms in only a 2x2 chunk (4 total chunks) inside their claim,if farms are built over this limit,they will be removed.
● If one of these farms causes immense server lag, we will remove the farm or ask for operation to cease.


► Farms:
● Hacks of all kind are not permitted on this server,More can be found here


Chat Rules:

These Chat Rules apply on Discord & the MinecraftFreedom Server

► Spam:
● Not Permitted - Flooding chat with characters , or the overuse of capitals or unicode.


► Links:
● Permitted - The sharing of links that are affiliated with the server , IE our Discord Link , Donation Link , Map Link or Website Link.
● Not Permitted - The sharing of any link that is not affiliated with the server.


► Sensitive Information:
●Not Permitted - The consensual or unconsensual release of a players sensitive information (Address , Telephone Number , FullName , IP Address Ect...) is not allowed under any circumstances.


► Privacy Violation/Doxxing:
●Permitted - You are allowed to make information such as your nationality , age or first name public.
●Non Permitted - You are not allowed to send, threaten via blackmail any personal information of another player.


► Influencing Spam:
●Permitted - Yiou can ask players to spam in their own messages (e.g. "First person to DM me 123 gets 100 money")
●Not Permitted - Any messages incitng players to spam outside of their own messages is not allowed.


► Malicious Links:
●Anything considered 18+ , malicious or illegal is not allowed.


► Inappropriate Chat:
● Not Permitted - Any Topics that would be considered 18+ , illegal , NSFW Content is not permitted in central server chats


► Server Threats:
● Not Permitted - Any threat made against the server , DDoS , Lag Machines , blackmail is not permitted.


► Promiting Self-Harm & Inappropriate Threats:
● Not permitted - Any encouragement of self harm , innappropriate threats or other threats to people is not allowed.


► Excessive Usage of Racial slurs & Anti Semetism:
● Not Permitted - Persistant use of slurs that are deemed offensive is not allowed.


► Harrasment:
