Commands

This will contain all the essential commands that you will become acquainted with.:
► /AFK- This will annouce your player as "afk."
► /bal- This will show your accounts balance.
► /bal "player"- This will show the balance of another account.
► /colors-Will show all avaliable spigot color codes.
► /compass "player"-This will highlight the direction of a player within your compass.
► /helpop "reason"--This will notify a online staff member, gaining their attention to assist you.
► /home "name"-This will allow you to teleport to one of your homes,
► /homes-Will open up a GUI of all your created homes.
► /delhome "name"-Deletes home from home list
► /ignore "name"- Will ignore all messages from a player.
► /lastonline "name"- Will show when a player was last online.
► /mail "player" "messgage"- Will send a mail to a player.
► /mail clear - Clears mail sent to you.
► /mail read-Will read given mail.
► /msg "player" "msg"-Send a private message to a player.
► /msg toggle-Disables private messages.
► /note "note"-Creates a private not for you to read.
► /pay "player" "amount"- Sends player a set amount of money.
► /ping-Checks players ping.
► /playtime-Checks amount of time you have spent on the server.
► /tpa "player"- Sends a teleporation request to a player.
► /tpaccept- Accepts teleportation request from a player.
► /tpadeny- Denies a teleporation request from a player.
► /spawn- Teleports you to a server spawn.
► /randomteleport-Randomly teleports you to somewhere on the servers map.
► /reply "message"-Replies to most recent message sent to you.
► /recipe "item"-Shows Items Craft Guide.
► /warp "name"-Warps to that warp of choice.
