Make A Chest Shop

Step One: Make a sign

Step Two: Place the item you wish to buy/sell inside the chest.

Step Three: Place your sign on or near your chest and type in the following info:

Line 1: Leave blank since this will automatically fill in with your username.

Line 2: Set the quantity of the item you wish to sell.

Line 3: B for Buy or S for Sell and the price

Examples:

B 10
or
S 5

The number of this value is of the servers offical currency

Line 4: Type in either ID or the name of the item you wush to sell.

Click "DONE" on the sign

Once done, if this was sucessfully created, a message will appear of this sucess, noting that you have sucessfully created the chestshop. Tips - You can have a Buy (B) price and a Sell (S) price on the same sign - To Buy and Sell on the same sign, separate the prices with a colon. Example: B 10:S 5
